Measurement of High Resistance Using Loss of Charge Method


DC POWER SUPPLY

STEP 1 : Make Connections as per the instructions given below:

1.    P1 of Power supply - P3 of Circuit Diagram

2.    P2 of Power supply - P4 of Circuit Diagram

3.    M1 of Multimeter - M3 of Circuit Diagram

4.    M2 of Multimeter - M4 of Circuit Diagram

NOTE : If wire is wrongly connected, Click on node number to detach the wire.

STEP 2 : Click on "CHECK" Button for checking the connections.    

STEP 3 : Turn ON Power supply by clicking on the Power button and set the voltage to 30V.

STEP 4 : Turn ON Switch 1 for charging the capacitor.

STEP 5 : Now, Turn ON Switch 2 for discharging the capacitor.

STEP 6 : Click on "START" Button to start the Timer & readings will add to the table automatically until the timer stops.

STEP 7 : Click on "PLOT" Button to plot the graph.

STEP 8 : Click on "PRINT" Button to print the webpage.

STEP 9 : Click on "RESET" Button to refresh the webpage.

FORMULA

1.  Find R (High Resistance)

       `R` = `(0.434t)/(Clog(V/v))` , C = 330µF

Where, V = Maximum Charging Voltage(30V)
             v = Discharging Voltage
